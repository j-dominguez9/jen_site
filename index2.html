Meet The Practitioner

Jennifer Hagelston, LCSW, Psychotherapy in New Haven, CT

My experience as a psychotherapist enables me to offer effective outpatient, individualized, psychological care. I treat a number of mental health disorders, including depression and anxiety, Aspergers and autism spectrum disorders, trauma and PTSD, and relationship issues. I provide a neutral, empathic setting for individuals, families, and couples to work through their issues.

Find out more

My Clinical Psychology Focus

My focus is to help individuals heal, energize, and become aware of their inner strengths. I achieve this by providing a neutral safe space, listening to your concerns, and customizing a psychotherapy treatment plan. 

Find out more

My Patient Promise

I promise to be there for you every step of your journey. My goal is to help you grow from your struggles, heal from your pain, and move forward to where you want to be in your life.
